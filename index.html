Sourya Basu

Brief bio

I am Sourya Basu, a final year graduate student in the Department of Electrical & Computer Enginerring at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I am really fortunate to be advised by Prof. Lav Varshney. I received my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering, with a minor in Artificial Intelligence in June, 2017 from IIT Kanpur. I spent the summer of 2016 at Information and Intelligence Group, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Prof. Lav Varshney on compression of graphical structures.
My research interests are broadly in the area of geometric deep learning, natural language processing, and information theory.
